Guide to Downloading Your Instagram Data

Follow these steps to request and download your Instagram data for the past 3 months:

  1. Log in to Instagram: Open the Instagram app or visit Instagram.com.
  2. Navigate to Privacy Settings:
    • On your profile, select Settings > Meta Accounts Center > Your Information and Permissions, then click on Download Your Information.
    • Press the button labeled "Download or Transfer Information". Then:
      • Select the option to download all available information.
      • Under Date Range, choose 3 months.
      • Enter a valid email address to receive your data.
      • For the format, select JSON.
  3. Wait for Your Data: Instagram will email you a download link within 48 hours. Be sure to check your spam/junk folder if you don't see it.
  4. Download and Unzip the File: Save the file to your device, then right-click to unzip the file on your computer.

Once you have your data ready, come back here to start your journey!
