Research Background

This project was inspired by research in the fields of:

  • Digital Wellbeing: Understanding how social media usage patterns affect mental health and personal relationships
  • Self-Reflection Through Data: Using personal data to gain insights into behavior patterns and habits
  • Human-Computer Interaction: Designing interfaces that encourage mindful technology use

Key research that influenced this project includes studies on social comparison on social media, the impact of Instagram on self-esteem, and strategies for maintaining healthy digital boundaries.

Privacy & Data Security

Your privacy is our top priority. Here's what you should know about how we handle your data:

  • All data analysis is performed locally in your browser
  • We do not store or transmit any of your Instagram data or questionnaire responses
  • No personal information is collected or shared with third parties
  • Your data is only used to generate insights during your active session

For questions or concerns about data handling, please contact me at ethanluchs@vt.edu
